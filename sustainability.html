Sustainability - Seamless Integration

Certifications & Sustainability

We understand that sustainability is essential for the future of our planet, and we take this responsibility seriously. By choosing our products, you can feel confident that you're choosing a company that values sustainability and environmental responsibility.

Eco Friendly Fibres

Our company is committed to integrating sustainable practices into every aspect of our operations. One of the ways we do this is by using eco-friendly fibres, such as Regenerated Cellulose, in our products. This type of fibre is produced from natural materials, reducing the environmental impact of our products and helping promote a greener future.

Smart Technology Yarns

We also use state-of-the-art technology in our spinning process to create sustainable yarns. Our MVS Vortex Spinning system is designed to minimise waste and reduce energy usage, ensuring that our products are made with care for the environment.

Seamless Integration

Sustainability is at the forefront of our operations and is seamlessly integrated into every step of the production process. This commitment to sustainability ensures that our products are not only environmentally friendly but also of the highest quality.

Renewable Energy

Our spinning system is powered by 85% renewable energy sources, including wind and solar power. This ensures that our operations are not dependent on non-renewable resources and reduces our carbon footprint.

Air Jet Weaving

Our Airjet weaving and modern processes are designed to produce clean fabrics with a longer lifespan. This reduces waste and promotes sustainability by ensuring that our products have a long and useful life.
